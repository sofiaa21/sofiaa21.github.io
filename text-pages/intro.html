Introduction

Did the recession reshape crime in San Francisco?

The 2008 financial crisis was one of the most impactuful economic catastrophies of recent history. Leaving thousands of Americans jobless and homeless, the crisis left the country in a state of tumoltuous peril it has been trying to come back from for almost two decades.

This was an occassion that touched every aspect of life of a regular person. Rising house prices, inflation and unemployment were some of the many consequences of the crisis. When such impactful situations happen that push many people into poverty and insecurity, it is common that criminal rates spike. We will be investigating the criminal impact of the 2008 recession in San Francisco, and the decade of uncertainty that followed.
